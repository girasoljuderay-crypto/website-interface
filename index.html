INTERACTIVE MAP FOR AUJRC

WELCOME TO ARELLANO UNIVERSITY JOSE RIZAL CAMPUS INTERACTIVE MAP

This interactive map is designed to help students, faculty, and visitors navigate the Arellano University Jose Rizal Campus with ease. Whether you're looking for specific buildings, offices, or facilities, this map provides a user-friendly interface to locate them quickly.

Features of the Interactive Map:

  • Search Functionality: Easily search for buildings and offices by name or category.
  • Zoom and Pan: Navigate through the campus map with zooming and panning capabilities for a closer look at specific areas.
  • Building Information: Click on buildings to view detailed information, including office hours, contact details, and services offered.
  • Directions: Get step-by-step directions to your desired location within the campus.
  • Accessibility Features: Information on accessible routes and facilities for individuals with disabilities.

We hope this interactive map enhances your experience at Arellano University Jose Rizal Campus. Feel free to explore and make the most of the resources available!
